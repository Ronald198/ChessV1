knight

sulmon ne forme L-je

bishop
leviz ne forme diagonaleje
sulmon ne forme diagonaleje
nuk leviz siper figurave te tjera
bllokon check nga bishop/rook/queen

rook
leviz ne menyre drejtvizore
sulmon ne menyre drejtvizore
nuk leviz siper figurave te tjera
bllokon check nga bishop/rook/queen

queen
union rook + bishop
bllokon check nga bishop/rook/queen

king

can castle nqs ska levizur as mbreti as rook i caktuar por:
	cant castle under check
	cant castle if pieces across the way
	cant castle if king must cross under a checked square
